Freckers
Each player can move one piece per turn, and only diagonally along the dark tiles.

Unless a piece has been made a king, it could only move in the same vertical direction.

Unless jumping over an opponent's piece, a player's piece can move diagonally only one tile.

If jumping over an opponent's piece, the player's piece can and must leap two tiles diagonally.

If a combination of jumps are available to the same piece in the same vertical direction, a player's piece can make multiple hops in the same turn.

When a piece reaches the opposite end of the board, it is converted into a king.

A king can move diagonally in either of the two vertical directions, and jump opponent pieces with the same freedom.

To win the game, a player must either: eliminate all of the opponents' pieces,

... or trap the opponent in such a way that they have no valid moves to make during their turn.
